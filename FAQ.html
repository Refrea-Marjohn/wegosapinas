Find answers to common questions about traveling in the Philippines

Booking a trip is easy! Simply browse our destinations or packages, select your preferred option, and click the "Book Now" button. Follow the step-by-step process to choose your dates, add any extras, and complete your payment securely.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, GCash, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

International travelers need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond their stay. Depending on your nationality, you may also need a visa. We recommend checking the latest requirements on the official Philippine Bureau of Immigration website.
