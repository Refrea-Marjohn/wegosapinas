Emergency Guides

What To Do In Case Of...

  1. Call 911 or go to the nearest hospital
  2. Contact your travel insurance provider
  3. Keep all medical receipts for insurance claims
  4. Inform your embassy if necessary
  5. Contact family members

Safety Tips

General Safety
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on your phone
  • Share your itinerary with family/friends
  • Keep digital copies of important documents
  • Know your hotel's emergency procedures
Health Safety
  • Carry basic first aid supplies
  • Know your blood type and allergies
  • Carry medical insurance information
  • Stay hydrated and use sun protection
Travel Safety
  • Use registered transportation services
  • Keep valuables in hotel safe
  • Be aware of local customs and laws
  • Monitor weather and travel advisories
